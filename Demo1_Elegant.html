The Journey

Our Story

From the bustling streets of Central to the quiet shores of Sai Kung...

We first met in Hong Kong on a rainy evening, sharing an umbrella at the Star Ferry pier in Tsim Sha Tsui. What started as a chance meeting under the city lights quickly blossomed into a profound connection, built on weekend hikes up Sunset Peak, shared milk teas, and quiet walks along the coastline.

Adrian proposed on a beautiful crisp evening in autumn, looking out over the panoramic skyline of our beloved city. With the harbor sparkling below us, Chloe said "Yes!" to a lifetime of adventures together.

We cannot wait to write our next chapter and celebrate our love with all of our amazing friends and family from Hong Kong and beyond!
